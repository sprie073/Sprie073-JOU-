Soccer unites the world together

It is a sport that is played globally whether the ball that is used is replaced with a sock, a rock, a shoe, a football, and or a basketball etc. The concept of kicking a spherical object into a net remains the same for everybody, no matter what corner of the world you're from.

Objects from left to right (Puma shoe,rocks, soccer ball,basketball, pair of stance socks)

It helped me realize my passion

Through soccer i have been able to realize my passion and what really moves me. It has also strongly helped me decide what career i want to pursue and have. Due to what i consider my healthy obssesion a day doesn't go by that i'm not watching a game or talking about it at the very least. I now aspire to become a sports journalist whether as an analyst on live tv or simply writing a recap for a game or an article on a player or team.
