Early Career

My career as a footballer started when I joined the Gloucester Hornets in 2009 and according to the coaches there was an obvious gap in skill between myself and the other 9 yr olds and as a result they put me in a league with kids two years older than I was. That was only for one season however as the next year i played with my age group and some games with the age groups above mine.

(Player playing soccer on field kicking white ball in black jersey)

Cumberland Cobras

I then made the decision to switch teams as i wanted to play at even more competitve level. I was given that oppurtunity with the Cumberland Cobras which is now known as OTFC (Ottawa-Toronto FC). I moved to the Cumberland Cobras when i was 12 years old. I played for the Cobras for nearly 6 years. I continued to play in my favourite position (left wing) and kept the jersey number #20 at both teams that i was at.The highlight of my time with the Cobras would be when my team and i competed in the Ontario Summer Games in 2016 as it was a tournament with a lot more prestige than i was accustomed to.

(Player playing soccer in white jersey with white ball at feet against yellow team)

High School Soccer Team

The last part of my playing career is me playing for both the futsal team in grades 7 and 8 and the school soccer team from grades 9-12.It was a very turbluent experience particularily in the beginning as i went from being one of the more mediocore players on the team to being the co captain of a winning team in my penultimate year of highschool. It was a bit surreal to me because my brother had also played on the team when he was in highschool and i was eager to follow his footsteps.
